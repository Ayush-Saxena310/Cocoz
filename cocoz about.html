Our Story

The idea of Cocoz began with a simple yet memorable experience. One day while exploring a new place, we came across a small coconut drink shop. Curious to try something refreshing,energetic we ordered a coconut shake. The moment we took the first sip, we experienced something truly special — a drink that was natural, refreshing,energetic,and full of tropical flavor.

That simple moment sparked an idea. We realized that such a refreshing and natural drink deserved to be shared with more people around the world. Inspired by that experience, Cocoz was created with the vision of bringing the authentic taste of coconut beverages to everyone.

Today, Cocoz represents freshness, simplicity,energetic and the joy of natural flavors. Every drink we create carries the same inspiration that started our journey — a single refreshing sip that we wanted to share with the world.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a globally loved brand that brings the natural goodness of coconuts to people everywhere. We aim to redefine refreshment by creating beverages that are healthy, natural,energetic and inspired by the pure taste of tropical ingredients.

Our Mission

At Cocoos, our mission is to craft refreshing coconut beverages using high-quality natural ingredients while delivering an enjoyable and healthy drinking experience. We are committed to spreading freshness, promoting natural choices, and sharing the vibrant taste of coconuts with the world.
