Our Leadership

Meet the people behind Cocoz

Ayush Saxena

Founder & CEO

Ayush Saxena is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Cocoz. He graduated from the National Institute of Design (NID).

With a passion for creativity, entrepreneurship, and natural beverages, Ayush founded Cocoz with a vision to share the refreshing taste of coconut drinks with people everywhere.

Anurag Saxena

Co-Founder & Operations Lead

Has done his B.ba from Jagran College of Arts and Commerce.

Apart from his academic pursuits, Anurag is also a dedicated chess player and has proudly represented Chhatrapati Sahuji Maharaj University in the North Zone Chess Tournament. His strategic mindset and leadership skills contribute greatly to the growth and management of Cocoz.
