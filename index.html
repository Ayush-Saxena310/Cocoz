About Cocoz

Cocoz delivers refreshing tropical coconut beverages, crafted with natural ingredients to energize and delight every taste.

Brand Story

The concept of Cocoz started during a trip where the founder experienced an unforgettable fresh coconut drink. Inspired by its natural taste and health benefits, the idea was born to bring this tropical refreshment to everyone worldwide, creating Cocoz as a brand committed to quality and flavor.

Vision

To become the leading brand for natural, healthy, and delicious coconut beverages worldwide, inspiring a lifestyle of freshness and vitality.

Mission

To craft high-quality, natural coconut drinks with innovative flavors, sustainable practices, and consistent care for every customer experience.

Leadership

Our CEO, Ayush Saxena, graduated from NID and leads the vision of Cocoz. Anurag Saxena, co-founder and manager, ensures smooth operations and product excellence while studying at Jagran College of Arts and Commerce.
